## 1. WHO WE ARE

Abot Kamay Digital Inc. ("AKD," "we," "us," or "our") is a One Person Corporation registered in the Philippines that operates a free WiFi advertising network ("Network" or "Platform") at various partner locations across the Philippines.

AKD is the Personal Information Controller (PIC) responsible for your personal data collected through the Platform, as defined under Republic Act No. 10173 (Data Privacy Act of 2012).

**Data Protection Officer (DPO):** [DPO Name] | [DPO Email] | [DPO Phone]

## 2. WHAT DATA WE COLLECT

| Data Type | What It Is | When Collected |
|-----------|-----------|----------------|
| Device Identifier (MAC Address) | A unique hardware identifier for your device | When you connect to the Network |
| Assigned IP Address | The temporary internet address assigned to your device | When you connect |
| Session Data | Date and time of connection, session duration, data volume | During each Network session |
| Ad Interaction Data | Which advertisements you viewed, whether you completed viewing | During ad viewing on the Portal |
| Device Information | Device type, operating system version | When you connect |
| Optional Registration Data | Mobile number or email address, if you voluntarily register | Only if you create an account |

**What we do NOT collect:** We do not collect your name, national ID, banking information, precise location, browsing history after you connect to the internet, or app usage data beyond what is needed to run the ad portal.

## 3. WHY WE COLLECT YOUR DATA (LEGAL BASIS)

| Purpose | Data Used | Legal Basis |
|---------|-----------|-------------|
| Providing the Network (granting WiFi access after ad viewing) | Device ID, session data, ad interaction data | Performance of service / Consent (via Portal Terms) |
| Fraud prevention and security monitoring | Device ID, IP address, session data | Legitimate interest; RA 10175 compliance |
| Billing advertisers accurately | Aggregate ad view counts (anonymized) | Legitimate interest |
| Compliance with Philippine law | Connection logs, device ID | Legal obligation |
| Service notifications (if registered) | Mobile number / email | Consent |

## 4. HOW LONG WE KEEP YOUR DATA

| Data Type | Retention Period | Reason |
|-----------|-----------------|--------|
| Connection Logs (device ID, IP, timestamps) | 180 days (up to 12 months if required by law) | Security, fraud prevention, RA 10175 cooperation |
| Ad Interaction Data | 24 months | Advertiser billing, revenue reporting, audit |
| Optional Registration Data | Duration of account + 12 months | Service delivery |
| Security Incident Records | 5 years | Legal compliance, NPC obligations |

## 5. WHO WE SHARE YOUR DATA WITH

We do not sell personal data to third parties for marketing purposes. We may share limited data with:

| Recipient | What Is Shared | Why |
|-----------|---------------|-----|
| Venue Partners (site operators) | None — Partners do not receive personal data from the Platform | N/A |
| Advertisers | Aggregate, anonymized campaign performance data only | Advertiser billing and reporting |
| Cloud / hosting providers (sub-processors) | Platform data stored securely | Infrastructure operation |
| Philippine law enforcement / government | Connection logs and session data, as required | Legal obligation (RA 10175, court orders) |
| NPC | Breach notifications, compliance reports | Legal obligation under RA 10173 |

## 6. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our Network is available to the public, including persons under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under thirteen (13) years of age without verifiable parental or guardian consent. If you believe we have collected your child's data, contact our DPO at [DPO Email] for immediate deletion. We implement content restrictions to protect minors from age-inappropriate advertising.

## 7. YOUR RIGHTS UNDER RA 10173

| Right | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Right to be Informed** | You have the right to know what personal data we collect, why, and how — which is the purpose of this Privacy Policy. |
| **Right of Access** | You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you by contacting our DPO. |
| **Right to Rectification** | If your personal data is inaccurate or incomplete, you may request correction. |
| **Right to Erasure** | You may request deletion where data is no longer necessary, subject to legal retention requirements. |
| **Right to Object** | You may object to processing for direct marketing or where processing is based on legitimate interest. |
| **Right to Data Portability** | Where technically feasible, you may request a machine-readable copy of your personal data. |

To exercise any right, contact our DPO at [DPO Email]. We respond within fifteen (15) Business Days. To file a complaint with the NPC: info@privacy.gov.ph | privacy.gov.ph | (02) 8234-2228.

## 8. DATA SECURITY

We implement the following measures to protect your data:

- Encryption of data in transit (TLS/HTTPS)
- Access controls and authentication for administrative systems
- Regular security assessments and vulnerability monitoring
- Physical security of server infrastructure
- Staff training on data privacy obligations

## 9. COOKIES AND TRACKING

Our captive portal may use session cookies solely to maintain your authenticated session and track ad viewing completion. We do not use cross-site tracking cookies, advertising tracking pixels from third parties, or persistent tracking identifiers. Third-party advertisers are prohibited from embedding tracking pixels or data collection tools without AKD's prior written approval.

## 10. LAW ENFORCEMENT REQUESTS

We may disclose personal data to Philippine law enforcement, courts, or government authorities when required by:

- A lawful court order or subpoena
- A valid law enforcement request under RA 10175
- Any applicable Philippine law

We will notify you of such disclosure where legally permissible.

## 11. CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified via Portal notice and the "Effective Date" above will be updated. Continued use of the Network after any change constitutes acceptance.
