## What We Collect

When you connect to this network, we automatically collect:

- Device MAC address and IP address
- Session start time, duration, and data usage
- Connection venue/site identifier

If you use optional login features (email or social login), we may also collect your email address or social login information.

## Why We Collect It

- To provide and manage your internet access session
- For network security and abuse prevention
- For advertising display and analytics
- To comply with applicable Philippine law

Data is retained for sixty (60) days and then deleted or anonymized, unless required by law.

## Advertising

This service may be supported by advertising. You may be required to view an advertisement before or during your session.

## Security Notice

Public WiFi networks may not be fully secure. Avoid transmitting sensitive personal or financial information — including passwords, banking credentials, or payment card details — while connected to this network. Use of a VPN is recommended for sensitive communications.

## Your Rights

Under the Data Privacy Act of 2012, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. Contact us at privacy@abotkamay.net.
