# Abot Kamay WiFi — Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 17, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

Abot Kamay WiFi (the "Company") respects and protects the privacy of all users who access our WiFi network. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information in connection with your use of our service.

This policy is issued in compliance with Republic Act No. 10173, the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (DPA), and the implementing rules and regulations issued by the National Privacy Commission (NPC) of the Philippines.

By connecting to our WiFi network, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and consent to the practices described herein.

We do not sell, rent, or trade personally identifiable information to any third party under any circumstances.

## 2. Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data on the following legal bases under the Data Privacy Act of 2012:

- **Consent** — Personal data is processed based on your freely given, informed, and specific consent obtained through the captive portal login page at the time of connection.
- **Legitimate Interests** — Data processing is also carried out to the extent necessary for the legitimate interests of operating, securing, and improving the WiFi network service, provided such interests are not overridden by your fundamental rights and freedoms.
- **Legal Obligation** — Data may be retained or disclosed where required by applicable Philippine law, regulation, or lawful order from a competent authority.

You may withdraw consent at any time by discontinuing use of the service. Withdrawal of consent does not affect the lawfulness of processing carried out prior to withdrawal.

## 3. Information We Collect

### 3.1 Device Information (Collected Automatically)

- Device MAC address
- IP address assigned to your device
- Device type and operating system
- Browser type and version

### 3.2 Network Usage Data (Collected Automatically)

- Session start time and end time
- Session duration
- Volume of data transmitted and received
- Connection location (venue/site identifier)

### 3.3 Optional User Information

The following data is collected only if you choose to use optional login features:

- Email address
- Mobile phone number
- Social media login data (if social login is used)

## 4. Purpose of Data Collection

We collect data solely for the following legitimate purposes:

- Providing and managing internet access sessions
- Network security monitoring and abuse prevention
- Detecting and preventing illegal or unauthorized activities
- System performance analysis and service improvement
- Displaying advertisements and measuring advertising performance
- Compliance with applicable Philippine law and regulatory requirements

Data will not be used for purposes other than those stated above without your additional consent.

## 5. Advertising and Third-Party Analytics

Our WiFi service may display advertisements before or during your internet access session. Advertising may be served by third-party advertising networks or analytics providers. These partners may collect limited, anonymized usage data for purposes including:

- Ad performance measurement
- Campaign reach and frequency analytics
- User engagement metrics

Third-party advertising partners operate under their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for the data practices of third-party partners. We do not sell personally identifiable information to third parties.

You may be subject to the privacy policies of third-party ad networks whose advertisements are displayed through our service.

## 6. Data Retention

Network logs and related personal data will be retained for a period of sixty (60) days from the date of collection. After this period, data will be permanently deleted or irreversibly anonymized, unless:

- Retention is required by applicable Philippine law or regulation, or
- Retention is ordered or requested by a competent law enforcement authority or court.

## 7. Data Security

We implement reasonable and appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include:

- Encrypted data transmission where technically feasible
- Restricted access to databases containing personal data
- Monitoring systems for unauthorized access attempts

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

## 8. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade personally identifiable information to third parties. We may disclose personal data only in the following circumstances:

- To authorized service providers and technology partners who support the operation of our network, subject to confidentiality obligations
- To advertising partners, in anonymized or aggregated form only
- To law enforcement authorities or government agencies when required by a lawful order, subpoena, or applicable regulation
- In connection with a corporate merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, subject to the same privacy obligations

## 9. International Data Transfers

Some of our advertising or analytics service providers may be located outside the Philippines. Where personal data is transferred internationally, we take reasonable steps to ensure such transfers comply with applicable data protection standards and the requirements of the Data Privacy Act of 2012.

## 10. Minors

This service is not directed to individuals under the age of eighteen (18). By connecting to our WiFi network, you represent that you are at least eighteen (18) years of age, or that you have obtained verifiable consent from a parent or legal guardian.

If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a minor under 18 without appropriate consent, we will take steps to delete such data promptly.

## 11. User Rights

Under the Data Privacy Act of 2012 and applicable NPC regulations, you have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

- **Right to be Informed** — to know how your data is collected and used
- **Right to Access** — to request a copy of your personal data we hold
- **Right to Correction** — to request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Right to Erasure or Blocking** — to request deletion or blocking of your data under certain circumstances
- **Right to Object** — to object to the processing of your data on legitimate grounds
- **Right to Data Portability** — to receive your data in a usable electronic format
- **Right to Lodge a Complaint** — with the National Privacy Commission

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 13. We will respond to your request within thirty (30) days.

## 12. NPC Complaint Procedure
