# ABOT KAMAY DIGITAL INC. — DATA BREACH RESPONSE POLICY

**AKD-POL-002 | RA 10173 · NPC Circular 16-03**
**Version 1.0 — Internal Policy**

Republic of the Philippines

---

## 1. PURPOSE AND SCOPE

This Policy establishes AKD's procedures for identifying, containing, investigating, notifying, and recovering from personal data breaches, in compliance with Republic Act No. 10173 and NPC Circular No. 16-03.

**Applies to:** all AKD employees and contractors; all Venue Partners; all third-party service providers; and all systems, devices, and locations where Platform data is processed or stored.

## 2. DEFINITION OF A PERSONAL DATA BREACH

A personal data breach is any incident leading to unauthorized acquisition, access, use, disclosure, alteration, destruction, or loss of personal data. Examples include:

- Unauthorized access to AKD's server or database containing end-user data
- Physical theft or loss of Equipment containing connection logs or session data
- Ransomware or cyberattack affecting Platform systems
- Accidental exposure of personal data to unauthorized parties
- Venue Partner employee accessing Platform data without authorization
- Third-party sub-processor security incident affecting AKD data

## 3. BREACH SEVERITY CLASSIFICATION

| Level | Description | NPC Notification | Response Timeline |
|-------|-------------|------------------|-------------------|
| **Critical** | Large-scale breach; sensitive data exposed; likely serious harm | Required within 72 hours | Immediate |
| **High** | Significant breach; 50+ individuals; moderate harm potential | Required within 72 hours | Within 4 hours |
| **Medium** | Limited breach; small number of individuals; low harm potential | Assess for notification | Within 24 hours |
| **Low** | Technical incident with no actual personal data exposure | Document internally | Within 48 hours |

## 4. BREACH RESPONSE TIMELINE

### Hour 0: Discovery

Any employee, contractor, or Venue Partner who discovers or suspects a breach shall immediately report to: [DPO Email] / [DPO Mobile]. Do not attempt to resolve independently. Do not communicate about the breach externally before reporting.

### Hours 0–4: Containment and Initial Assessment

- DPO assembles breach response team.
- Identify and isolate affected systems or Equipment.
- Stop ongoing unauthorized access without destroying evidence.
- Preserve logs, screenshots, and evidence.
- Initial classification: Critical / High / Medium / Low.
- Notify AKD management immediately for Critical/High breaches.

### Hours 4–24: Investigation and Documentation

- Determine: what data was affected; how many individuals; cause and vector; potential harm.
- Complete internal Breach Incident Report.
- Notify affected Venue Partners if breach originated at or affects a Deployment Site.
- If Equipment theft: file police report; document with photos.

### NPC 72-Hour Rule

For breaches likely to give rise to a real risk of serious harm, AKD shall notify the NPC within 72 hours using the NPC's prescribed notification form. Submit to: info@privacy.gov.ph

### Day 2–30: Full Report and Remediation

- Submit full breach report to NPC within thirty (30) days of initial notification.
- Implement remediation: patch vulnerabilities, update access controls, replace compromised Equipment if needed.
- Conduct post-incident review.
- Archive all breach documentation for minimum five (5) years.

## 5. VENUE PARTNER OBLIGATIONS

| Situation | Venue Partner Action | Timeline |
|-----------|---------------------|----------|
| Unauthorized Equipment access or tampering | Immediately notify AKD DPO; preserve scene; file police report if criminal act suspected | Within 1 hour |
| Equipment theft | File police report; notify AKD; provide case reference number | Within 24 hours |
| Unusual Platform behavior suggesting breach | Notify AKD DPO with screenshots and description; do not shut down Equipment without AKD instruction | Within 4 hours |
| Government or law enforcement data request | Notify AKD immediately; do not provide data without AKD's legal guidance | Immediately |
